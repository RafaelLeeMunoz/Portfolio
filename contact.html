Common Questions

FAQ

What types of projects do you take on? +

I work on product strategy engagements, game development projects (Unreal, Unity), brand identity work, AI integration consulting, and music composition for games and media. I take on a small number of high-quality projects at a time.

What does your typical engagement look like? +

Most project engagements start with a discovery call, followed by a scoped proposal. I work on monthly retainers for ongoing PM work, or fixed-scope contracts for game dev and design projects. I'm transparent about timelines and deliverables from day one.

Do you work with early-stage startups? +

Yes — early-stage is where I do some of my best work. I love the 0→1 phase and I've helped multiple founding teams go from idea to funded product. Budget flexibility is possible for the right project.

Can I just commission a piece of resin art or a music track? +

Absolutely! Custom resin commissions and music tracks are available — just indicate your interest in the form above. For resin pieces, include any color preferences or size requirements. For music, share the mood, genre, and use case.
