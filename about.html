My Story

From a kid obsessed with great experiences to building them at scale.

I was born in Brooklyn, NY and raised in New England, where I grew up driven by three things: helping people, building things, and video games. Those passions didn’t fade, they evolved.

Today, I bring together Product Management, Game Development, Creative Direction, and AI to design experiences that feel intuitive, impactful, robust, and memorable.

My career spans military service, indie game studios, enterprise product teams, and solo creative ventures. Along the way, I’ve shipped mobile apps, launched digital products across multiple states, built brands, composed music, and created physical products, often late at night with lo-fi playing in the background. Each discipline strengthens the others.

Today, I’m focused on the intersection of AI, wellness, and interactive tools, building intelligent systems that help teams move faster, think better, and punch above their weight.
