My Story

From a Kid Who Loved Games
to Building Them

I grew up in Rhode Island obsessed with two things: video games and making stuff. Those two passions never left me — they just grew up too. Today I combine product management, game development, creative direction, and AI to build experiences that surprise and stick.

My career has taken me through indie game studios, product teams at early-stage startups, brand agencies, and solo creative ventures. I've shipped mobile apps, launched brand identities, composed original music, and poured resin at 2am while listening to lo-fi beats. Each discipline feeds the others.

These days I'm focused on the intersection of AI and interactive storytelling — building tools that make game narratives feel alive, and helping small teams punch above their weight with smart product strategy.
