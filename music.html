Process

Behind the Music

I produce almost entirely in Ableton Live with a collection of hardware synths, a MIDI controller, and an unhealthy number of plugin subscriptions. My process starts with texture — finding a pad or a sample that sets the emotional tone — before melodies or structure come into play.

For game soundtracks, I work closely with the design team to map music to game states, using adaptive audio middleware like FMOD and MetaSounds to create music that breathes with the player's experience.
